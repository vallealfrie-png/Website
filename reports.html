External survey results: Closing summary

Respondents Demographic

For the external respondents' demographics highest percentage is in the bracket of 20-39 years old, 29% in female respondents, and 17% in male respondents.

Respondents Market Occupation

We interviewed 58% working individuals, 22% students, 13% pensioner, 6% househusband/housewifie and 1% unemployed.

Market Locations

We interviewed respondents located in Pulang Lupa, Bf Homes, Talon 1-5, Pamplona 1-2, Casimiro, Makati, Verdant, and Mandaluyong.

62% visited hospitals (Past 6 Months)

For the 100 respondents, 26% respondents answered PHMC-LP as their last visited hospital for the past 6 months, while 7% respondents answered Ultramax as their last visited hospital. The other remaining respondents answered LPGH, Christ the King, Callejo, Asian Hospital, PGH, and Hi-Precision as their last visited hospital for the past 6 months.

38% visited hospitals (Over 6 Months)

Surprisingly 6% answered that they never visited any hospital in their lives, 4% answered LPDH, 3% answered LPGH, and Pope John Hospital as their last hospital visited over the past 6 months. While PHMC-LP is mentioned by the 2% respondents as their last hospital visited over the past 6 months.

Closing remarks

The external survey results prove that PHMC-LP still has a lot of potential consumers that they can get in the Las Pinas area. Many consumers still never consider PHMC-LP as their first choice or top of mind. Clinics are the top choice of working individuals around Las Pinas, while others still consider mostly brgy. centers as their top choice since it is more convenient and near their homes.
